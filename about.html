About Our Company

Your Trusted Valuation Partner

Alwandeaphiwe (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned, B-BBEE level 1 contributor specializing in a comprehensive range of property valuations. We navigate the complexities of the ever-changing South African property market to provide you with innovative and constructive solutions.

Our commitment to excellence ensures we deliver the highest quality service, tailored to your specific needs. We pride ourselves on our integrity, professionalism, and dedication to providing accurate and reliable valuations that you can trust.

Our Mission

To provide our clients with accurate, reliable, and timely valuation services, while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of property valuation services in South Africa, recognized for our expertise, innovation, and commitment to client satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We adhere to the highest ethical standards in all our business dealings.
  • Professionalism: We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality of service.
  • Accuracy: We strive for precision and accuracy in all our valuations.
  • Client Focus: We are dedicated to meeting the unique needs of each of our clients.
