Our Valuation Services

Residential Valuations

Our expert team provides accurate and reliable valuations for all types of residential properties, including houses, apartments, townhouses, and vacant residential land. Whether for sale, purchase, mortgage, or financial reporting, we ensure a thorough assessment that reflects the true market value. We understand the nuances of the residential market and provide detailed reports tailored to your specific needs.

Municipal Valuations

We have extensive experience in compiling General and Supplementary Valuation rolls for both Metropolitan and Local Municipalities throughout South Africa. Our services include property valuations for municipal rates and taxes, objections to municipal valuations, and expert witness testimony in valuation disputes. We ensure compliance with relevant legislation and provide robust, defensible valuations.

Agricultural Valuations

Our specialist agricultural valuers offer expert advice for a variety of agricultural properties, including farms, smallholdings, and specialized agricultural enterprises. We consider factors such as land use, water rights, soil quality, infrastructure, and agricultural potential to provide comprehensive and accurate valuations for farming operations, land acquisition, and financial planning.

Commercial & Industrial Valuations

We provide in-depth knowledge and accurate valuations for a wide range of commercial and industrial properties, including office buildings, retail centers, warehouses, factories, and industrial parks. Our valuations are crucial for investment, financing, sale, and lease purposes, offering detailed analysis of market trends, income potential, and property specifics.

Specialized Property Valuations

Our team possesses unique expertise in valuing specialized properties that require a distinct approach, such as airports, hospitals, educational institutions, hotels, and leisure facilities. These valuations often involve complex income streams, operational considerations, and specific market dynamics, for which we provide tailored and insightful assessments.

Plant & Machinery Valuations

Beyond immovable property, we also offer valuations for plant, machinery, and equipment across various industries. This includes manufacturing equipment, agricultural machinery, and specialized industrial assets. These valuations are essential for financial reporting, insurance, asset disposal, and mergers and acquisitions.

Financial Reporting Valuations

We provide valuations for financial reporting purposes, adhering to International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and other relevant accounting standards. Our reports assist companies in accurately reflecting the value of their assets on their balance sheets, crucial for audits, mergers, acquisitions, and corporate restructuring.

Expropriation Valuations

Our valuers are experienced in providing independent valuations for expropriation purposes, ensuring fair compensation for property owners whose land is acquired for public use. We navigate the legal and valuation complexities involved, providing expert reports and advice to protect our clients' interests.

Feasibility Studies & Development Appraisals

We conduct comprehensive feasibility studies and development appraisals for proposed property developments. This includes market analysis, financial projections, risk assessment, and highest and best use analysis to determine the viability and potential profitability of a project before significant investment is made.
