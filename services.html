Commercial & Industrial Valuations

We provide in-depth knowledge and accurate valuations for a wide range of commercial and industrial properties, including office buildings, retail centers, warehouses, factories, and industrial parks. Our valuations are crucial for investment, financing, sale, and lease purposes, offering detailed analysis of market trends, income potential, and property specifics.

Key Benefits:

  • Comprehensive market analysis
  • Income potential assessment
  • Investment grade reporting
  • Lease advisory services

Residential Property Valuations

Our expert team provides accurate and reliable valuations for all types of residential properties, including houses, apartments, townhouses, and vacant residential land. Whether for sale, purchase, mortgage, or financial reporting, we ensure a thorough assessment that reflects the true market value.

What We Cover:

  • Market value assessments
  • Mortgage application support
  • Estate planning valuations
  • Insurance claim assessments

Agricultural Property Valuations

Our specialist agricultural valuers offer expert advice for a variety of agricultural properties, including farms, smallholdings, and specialized agricultural enterprises. We consider factors such as land use, water rights, soil quality, infrastructure, and agricultural potential.

Specialized Assessment:

  • Water rights evaluation
  • Infrastructure assessment
  • Agricultural potential studies
